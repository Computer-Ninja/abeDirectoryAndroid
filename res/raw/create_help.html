* Denotes a required field.

This guide will help you understand how to complete a business listing on Abe. Once you have checked all the details are correct, hit the submit button. You shall then recieve an email confirming your registration. The email will be sent to the email address you used to register on Abe.

PLEASE NOTE: The details you provide will be displayed to users, unless otherwise stated.

Business name * 
Enter a suitable business name i.e. Abe Ltd
Company registration no. (optional) 
Enter your company registration number from Companies House to verify your listing on Abe.
Your Company registration no. will never be shown to users!
Business category * 
Select a business category that best relates to your business or service.
Business type * 
Select a business type that best matches your business or service.
  • Store: if you want users to locate you and see your location on a map. (full address is required)
  • Online: if you trade solely online. (you must provide a valid web address i.e. http://www.abe.com)
  • Other: if you are unsure. (If you select this option you will need to provide at least one of the following to be displayed to users: 1) a valid address, 2) a valid email address, or 3) a valid contact no.)
Business summary * 
Tell users about your business (1000 characters max). Try to use words that users can search for i.e. If you sell babies clothes, include this here.
Trading since (date) (optional) 
Enter the date your business or service started trading. e.g. 01/01/2016
Address details 
Enter your business address details so that users can find you.

If you selected 'Sore' you need to provide a full address. Providing a full address allows users to find you and also allows users to see your business location on a map.
(These address details will be shown to users).
Contact details 
Enter your business contact details so that users can contact you.

If you selected 'Other', you need to provide at least one means of contact. i.e. email, contact no. or full address
(These contact details will be shown to users).
Referral code (optional) 
If you have recieved a Referral code from another business, registered on Abe, you may use it here.
(It will never be shown to users!)
